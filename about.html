This is a small business that seeks to revive and bring Japans Dagashiya craze from the 50's into the local area of Signal Hll~

This small business aims to bring Japan's Dagashiya craze from the 50's to a more modern world, first into the local area of Signal Hill, then eventually to a more international and possibly global outlet. The more items bought means the higher chance of the site to be able to successfully upgrade with more goods meaning MORE DAGASHI TO TRY! and MORE PLACES FOR THESE DAGASHI TO BE DELIVERED!
